About Kenyan meals

Staples are maize and other cereals depending on the region, including millet and sorghum eaten with various meats and vegetables. The foods that are universally eaten in Kenya are ugali, sukuma wiki, chapati, pilau and nyama choma. Kenya's coastal cuisine is unique and highly regarded throughout the country.
